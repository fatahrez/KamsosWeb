About Kamsos

Pastrolists and farmers will learn major livestock education like animal production, feed manufacturing, feed conservation, livestock marketing, value chain development, range management, disaster and risk reduction mitigation, Extension advisory on production systems, livestock breeding and increased productivity, livestock disease causes, prevention & treatment etc. online and get prescribed veterinary drugs online from the nearest agrovet through the site.

Kamsos' goal is transforming the traditional methodologies of veterinary and animal-production services provision.

Meet our Specialists

Ms Kowthar A. Mohamed

Kowthar is a dryland and animal scientist hailing from Garissa county, North Eastern region of Kenya. Her passion for helping pastrolists/farmers from marginalized areas to get any veterinary services easily, efficiently and free of charge contributed to the birth of Kamsos. Through her effort pastrolists & farmers are connected to veterinary doctors and centres such as animal health, animal feed, livestock market etc.
